About The Mission

Welcome! Connecting People Through Wrestling (CPTW) is dedicated to reminding people what it's like to be a kid again—to rediscover that feeling of pure joy, wonder, and being completely present in the moment.

My goal is to showcase the inherent connections that bind us all. On a deeper, spiritual level, we are all part of a universal tapestry. The roar of a wrestling crowd, the shared rhythm of a festival, the collective gasp at a stunning performance—these are powerful moments where thousands of individual energies sync up.

Professional wrestling and live music are modern-day rituals. They are sacred spaces for emotional release. By sponsoring this mission, you aren't just buying a ticket; you are supporting a movement to prove that we are all more connected than we are different.
